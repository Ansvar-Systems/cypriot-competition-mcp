# Coverage

## Authority

**Commission for the Protection of Competition (CPC-CY)**
- Website: https://www.competition.gov.cy/
- Jurisdiction: Cyprus (CY)
- Legal basis: Law 13(I)/2022 on the Protection of Competition

## What Is Covered

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Enforcement decisions | Abuse of dominance, cartel enforcement, and sector inquiries |
| Merger control | Phase I and Phase II merger control decisions |
| Sectors | Banking, telecommunications, energy, retail, tourism, construction, media |

## What Is NOT Covered

- Court appeals of CPC-CY decisions (handled by the Administrative Court of Cyprus)
- Informal guidance and comfort letters
- Leniency application details (confidential)
- Decisions predating digital publication on the CPC-CY website

## Data Currency

Data is ingested periodically from the CPC-CY official website. The `cy_comp_check_data_freshness` tool reports the current staleness status. For the most recent decisions, consult https://www.competition.gov.cy/ directly.

## Language

CPC-CY publishes decisions primarily in **Greek**, with selected decisions available in English. This server includes both Greek and English content where available.
